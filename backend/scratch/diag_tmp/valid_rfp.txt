RFP TITLE: Secure Local RFP Workspace
Client: Enterprise Procurement Department
Deadline: 30 June 2026

This request for proposal describes a secure local AI workspace for RFP analysis, retrieval, chat, draft generation, auditability, and document export. The bidder must keep all content local on a Windows laptop, use Ollama for chat and embeddings, support a clear stakeholder workflow, and provide exportable proof. The solution must not use cloud AI providers and must maintain an audit trail for every important action. The proposal should include timeline, delivery approach, risk management, security controls, assumptions, support model, and next steps.
